About the Old Course

The Old Course at St Andrews is considered the oldest and most iconic golf course in the world. For over 600 years, this hallowed ground has challenged golfers and shaped the very game itself. Today, it remains a public course, open to all who wish to walk in the footsteps of legends.

Course Overview

  • Par: 72
  • Length: 6,721 yards
  • Founded: circa 1400s
  • Type: Links
  • Designer: Nature/Time
