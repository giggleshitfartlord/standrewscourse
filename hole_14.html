Hole 14 - "Long"

Hole Overview

  • Par: 5
  • Length: 618 yards
  • Stroke Index: 3

Description

Aptly named "Long," this is one of the most challenging par 5s in championship golf. The hole features the infamous Hell Bunker on the second shot, and the approach must navigate the Beardies bunkers. A true three-shot hole for most players.

Strategy Tips

  • Drive must avoid Beardies bunkers on the right
  • Hell Bunker comes into play on second shot
  • Laying up short of Hell Bunker often wise
  • Green is well-protected - accuracy essential
  • Conservative play recommended in championship conditions
