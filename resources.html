Booking Your Round

Advanced Booking

The Old Course operates a ballot system for tee times:

  • Book 48 hours in advance through the Daily Ballot
  • Advanced reservations available (up to 1 year ahead)
  • Single golfers can check in at the Starter's Hut for possible openings
Check Daily Ballot

Green Fees and Payment

  • High Season (April-October): £290
  • Low Season (November-March): £180
  • Payment required at time of booking
  • Cancellation policy applies
